A Message for the Future of Our Bar

Muhammad Farooq Warind, Advocate Supreme Court, is a dedicated lawyer based in Rahim Yar Khan committed to restoring the integrity, prestige, and honor of the legal fraternity. He is also passionate about the implementation of human rights and upholding the rule of law. To advance his vision of an empowered bar, he is contesting the upcoming elections for Member Punjab Bar Council (Rahim Yar Khan Seat).
